Dockspace
Version 0.8 (08)
By Tangent Labs

Hi friends,

Dockspace began as a simple idea: the Dock should be more than a place to launch apps.

MacOS users spend countless hours looking at their Dock, yet it has remained largely unchanged for years. Dockspace brings new life to the Dock with beautiful widgets, customization, and thoughtful details designed to make your Mac feel more personal.

Built with care for the Mac community, Dockspace aims to feel right at home on your desktop, powerful when you need it, unobtrusive when you don't.

Thank you for being part of the journey.
